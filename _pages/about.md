Welcome! I am an economist at the [Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)](https://www.fao.org) of the United Nations, where I work on price incentives and food security.
I hold a Master's degree in Economics from the [Barcelona School of Economics](https://bse.eu) and a Bachelor's degree from [Universidad San Francisco de Quito (USFQ)](https://www.usfq.edu.ec). My research interests lie at the intersection of development economics, agricultural economics, and education.

I am originally from Ecuador.
